Key Findings:

• Sales grew steadily from 2018–2021 with strongest growth in 2021
• Technology category generated highest revenue but not highest margin
• Top 20% customers contributed ~80% of revenue (Pareto effect confirmed)
• High discount levels strongly correlate with negative profit
• West region produced highest profit concentration
• Several sub-categories show high sales but negative margins — discount risk
• Customer repeat purchase gaps vary widely — segmentation opportunity
